Why we exist

Aesthetics shouldn't
be a secret.

Most patients exploring aesthetic treatments are informed, budget-conscious, and ready to invest in themselves — they just can't get the information they need to make a confident decision. Pricing is hidden. Credentials aren't easy to find. Comparing providers means opening tab after tab hoping the information is even there.

Seeking Aesthetics closes the transparency gap. Every provider on our platform lists their starting prices, credentials, specific equipment, and treatment specialties — before a patient ever makes contact. Patients see their options, compare what matters, and choose who they want to call them.

No unsolicited calls. No price surprises. No guessing whether the injector is qualified for the treatment they want.

"When patients have real information, they make confident choices. That's better for everyone — especially the providers they choose."

The Skin Passport

Your treatment history.
Follows you everywhere.

Not all MedSpas offer every treatment. Not all providers have your history. Your Skin Passport solves both — a complete record that travels with you across every provider you ever see.

📍
Follows you to every provider
Switch MedSpas, try a new specialist, move cities — your complete treatment history goes with you. Your next provider already knows your skin before they meet you.
🔔
Alerts you on next steps
Botox wears off at 3–4 months. Filler at 12–18 months. Your passport tracks timing and reminds you before you notice the change — not after.
🌱
Fills the gaps between spas
No single spa does everything. Your passport shows what you've had done elsewhere so your current provider can treat your whole skin story — not just today's visit.
🔒
Owned by you. Always.
Most spa booking systems own your data. We don't. Your Skin Passport belongs to you — not the platform, not the spa, not a software subscription you might cancel someday.

The Transparency Test

Does the platform require providers to display verifiable credentials and standardized pricing — or just promotional content and glossy photos?

On Seeking Aesthetics, transparent pricing is a hard requirement — not a feature. Every provider lists per-unit, per-syringe, per-session pricing before any patient contact. No exceptions.

🗂️
✓ We Pass

The Data Ownership Test

Does the platform allow for a secure, portable, longitudinal patient health record — or does it keep your data siloed in a spa's booking system?

Your Skin Passport is patient-owned, portable, and permanent. It moves with you across every provider — on our platform or off it. Most platforms can't say that.

📋
◑ In Progress

The Compliance Test

Does the platform offer automated tracking of medical licenses, supervising physician protocols, and state safety logs — or is it just a directory?

We verify Utah DOPL licenses and medical oversight at onboarding. Automated renewal tracking and state safety log integration is in our next build phase — and we're being honest about that.

From the founder

Built for patients who
deserve real information.

chosen by the right patient.

I saw the same pattern everywhere. Patients who knew exactly what they wanted — Botox, a HydraFacial, Morpheus8 — spending hours trying to answer basic questions. What does it cost? Who's qualified? How do I know they're good at this treatment specifically? The information wasn't available in any useful form.

At the same time I was watching talented, experienced injectors struggle to reach the patients who would have loved them. Not because of their quality. Because of their marketing budget.

Seeking Aesthetics solves both at once. Patients see verified providers with transparent pricing and confirmed credentials. They compare side by side and choose who calls them. That provider calls within 30 minutes — already knowing the patient's treatment, goals, and budget. The conversation starts right.

I personally call every provider I bring onto this platform. Because this only works if the right people are part of it — and that starts with me.
