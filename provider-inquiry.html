A Note from the Founder

I found the gap.
Because I lived it.

I was that patient. I spent hours scrolling through social media pages, websites, and apps trying to find a provider I could actually trust. Pricing was hidden. Credentials were vague. Consultations were filled with false pretenses. I left more than one MedSpa feeling misled — and I still hadn't found what I was looking for. The experience of navigating the aesthetics world as a newcomer was exhausting, confusing, and unnecessarily hard.

But I also heard the other side. Providers spending thousands on ads, getting inquiries from people three states away who didn't understand geography settings. Scheduling consultations, showing up, and getting ghosted. Doing the work of marketing on top of the work of injecting — and still not knowing where next month's patients were coming from.

I found the gap. Not just for patients — for providers too. SeekingAesthetics exists because both sides deserve better. I am not a software company pretending to care about your practice. I answer my phone. I am personally involved in every onboarding. I replace bad leads without a fight. And I only succeed when you do — because your subscription is how this platform survives.

My personal commitment to every founding provider: I will call you within 48 hours of your application. I will walk you through setup personally. And if you ever receive a bad lead, text me directly — it will be replaced within 48 hours. No forms. No tickets. Just me.
